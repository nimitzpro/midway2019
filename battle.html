Order of Battle

Initial Air Attacks

At 04:30, Nagumo launches a strike against the Midway Islands, and launches 8 search aircraft from Tone. Simultaneously, 10 planes from USS Yorktown begin searching for the Japanese ships.

At 05:34, a PBY Catalina spots the Japanese Carriers.

At 06:20, Japanese carrier aircraft bombard Midway.
